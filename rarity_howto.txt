The rarity of a thing in SpaceGame is described by a special JSON data structure, the titular 'rarity' field. It is a JSON dictionary, consisting of two fields. The 'default' field is the fallback rarity of the item. The 'factions' field is a list of dictionaries, where each dictionary has a 'faction' field and a 'rarity' field. The rarity of a faction ovverides the default rarity of the thing if the thing is in a reigon controlled by the faction.
